How It Works

Precision-engineered. Assembled with you.

Every ClawForge deployment follows a deliberate, four-phase process — from understanding your business to handing you a fully operational AI command center. Nothing is rushed. Nothing is automated without your approval.

The Four Phases

From first call to full command.

This isn't a SaaS onboarding flow. It's a hands-on build process where every component is configured to your business — together, on camera or in person.

01
Phase One — Discovery

We Learn Your Business

Before we build anything, we need to understand what you actually do. We get on a Zoom call or meet in person and map out your operations — your workflows, your pain points, where you're losing time, and where AI agents will create the most leverage.

What happens: A candid, no-sales-pitch conversation about your business. We ask about your daily operations, the tools you currently use, your team structure, and what outcomes you actually care about. We take notes. We ask follow-up questions. By the end, we know exactly what your agent army needs to look like.
02
Phase Two — Architecture

We Design Your Agent Hierarchy

Based on what we learned, we architect your complete system — your orchestrator (the single point of command), the CEO-level agents that manage each department, and the sub-agents beneath them that handle execution.

What gets designed: Your orchestrator's personality and routing logic. Which CEO agents you need (Operations, Finance, Marketing, Sales, Customer Success, etc.). What automation workflows each agent runs. How they communicate with each other. What guardrails and oversight mechanisms prevent errors. How your security layer protects everything.
03
Phase Three — Live Build

We Build It Together, Face-to-Face

This is the core of the ClawForge experience. During your dedicated live session (60 minutes on Zoom or in person), we walk through every single piece of your agent army as it comes online. You see everything being built. You understand every agent. You approve every workflow before it goes live.

Core Setup (1 session): One focused 60-minute Zoom call or in-person meeting covers your full orchestrator build, agent deployment, Kanban + Telegram integration, 3 automation workflows, and a complete walkthrough of how to operate your system day-to-day.

Executive Build (2 sessions): Two 60-minute sessions give us room for advanced workflow orchestration, deeper security hardening, custom agent personality tuning, and more complex multi-agent coordination. The second session is pure optimization — fine-tuning based on how the system performs in your real environment.
04
Phase Four — Launch & Maintain

You Go Live. We Stay With You.

Once you're launched, we don't disappear. You become a ClawForge member — which means ongoing support, agent updates, workflow maintenance, and access to weekly community Zoom calls. The relationship between you, your orchestrator, and your sub-agents is something we actively maintain over time.

What ongoing looks like: Private support channel for async questions. Weekly live Zoom calls open to all members. Agent behavior updates as AI models improve. Workflow adjustments as your business evolves. Priority access to new agent types and features. Executive Build clients also get a 7-day post-launch optimization window with priority support.
Anatomy of a Deployment

What's inside your AI command center.

Every ClawForge build includes these core components — precision-fit to your specific business operations.

⚡

The Orchestrator

The brain. You speak to it in plain language and it routes your requests to the right agents, tracks progress across departments, and reports back with status updates. One conversation controls everything.

👔

CEO-Level Agents

Specialized executive agents that manage entire business functions — Operations, Finance, Marketing, Sales, Customer Success, and more. Each one runs its own workflows and manages its own sub-agents.

Automation Workflows

Pre-configured automation recipes that handle recurring tasks — invoice tracking, content scheduling, lead follow-ups, report generation, and whatever else your business runs on.

Kanban + Telegram

A visual task board for tracking agent work, plus Telegram integration so you can issue commands, get updates, and manage your army straight from your phone.

Your personal strategy agent. It brainstorms ideas, drafts documents, researches competitors, analyzes data, and helps you think through decisions — like a chief of staff that never sleeps.

Security Layer

Encrypted data handling, isolated environments, access controls, automated backups, and a Security Sentinel agent that monitors system integrity and alerts you to anomalies.
