Security & Data
Security is our foundation. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Your agent environment is fully isolated — no cross-contamination with other clients. Backups run automatically. Access is controlled through granular permissions. Everything is configured and verified by a human during your live session. Full security details →
We configure your system and have access during setup and maintenance. All access is transparent — you see exactly what we can touch during your live session, and permissions are set with your explicit approval. We treat your data with the same gravity we'd treat our own.
We build guardrails and oversight mechanisms into every deployment. Agents are configured to flag uncertain outputs rather than presenting them as fact. For critical business functions (financial data, legal matters), agents are set to surface information for your review rather than acting autonomously.
